How to play

👥Two teams — Red and Blue. Each team picks one Spymaster; everyone else guesses.
🔤25 words on the board. Some belong to each team — only spymasters see which.
💡The spymaster gives a one-word clue + a number, like OCEAN · 2, linking their team's words.
👆Guessers tap words. Right word = keep guessing. Wrong word = turn over.
💀Never tap the assassin word — instant loss! Find all your words first to win.

What is WordSpies?

WordSpies is a free online word game you play with friends in real time. Two teams — red and blue — race to find their secret words on a 5×5 board using one-word clues from their spymaster. No downloads, no accounts: create a room, share the 4-letter code or invite link, and play on any phone or laptop.

How many players do you need?

At least 4 — one spymaster and one guesser per team. It's even better with 6–10 players in the room.

Is WordSpies free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up, no install. Open the site, share your room code, and start playing.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes, WordSpies works on any modern phone, tablet or computer browser — and friends can mix devices in the same game.
